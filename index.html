Kick on football club & Academy

Welcome to kick on Football Club & Academy! The Kick on Football Club & Academy is an initiative aimed at identifying, nurturing, and promoting football talent at grassroots and elite levels. It will serve as a training, educational, and developmental hub for aspiring footballers, combining international coaching methodologies with modern infrastructure. The academy will also emphasize personal growth, discipline, and education, preparing athletes for careers both on and off the pitch.

Vision

To become a leading football development center that fosters talent, professionalism, and character, creating a pipeline of players capable of representing clubs, states, and national teams with pride and excellence.

Mission

To deliver world-class football training, infrastructure, and support to athletes while promoting values of integrity, discipline, and teamwork through the beautiful game.

Objectives

To provide structured football training to youth (ages UN3–UN19). • To identify and develop raw talent through certified coaches and modern techniques. • To establish partnerships with national and international football clubs. • To host and participate in local, regional, and international tournaments.
