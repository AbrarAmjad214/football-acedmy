Football Only Course at FCV International Football Academy

At FCV International Football Academy, we offer flexible football programs ranging from 2 weeks to a full season. You can join our Under 23’s squad, college teams, or our Semi-Professional Men’s team if you are truly outstanding. Our UEFA-qualified coaches provide personalised training, competitive fixtures, and comprehensive player development support.

Football Program

At FCV International Football Academy, we pride ourselves on providing a highly flexible football program that caters to your schedule and goals. Whether you’re looking for an intense 2-week course to improve quickly, or a comprehensive 1-year program for long-term development, we have options to suit all needs. This flexibility allows players to tailor their experience to match their commitment level and aspirations in the sport. Each week, players will have over 20 hours of football training and play in competitive matches every Wednesday.

Our daily training sessions are designed to challenge players and elevate their skills in all areas of the game. Our experienced UEFA-qualified coaches focus on improving technical abilities, tactical understanding, and overall performance, ensuring that each session is tailored to push players to the next level. Whether refining individual skills or working on team strategies, every aspect of your training is geared toward continuous improvement.

In addition to the high-level training, you will have the chance to compete in competitive matches against top academies and clubs across England. These real-game scenarios provide the perfect opportunity to test your skills and apply what you’ve learned in training.

Player Pathways

We pride ourselves on providing a proven pathway to success. Players join us from all over the world, and many have gone on to secure professional contracts with top clubs and play for their national teams. With our expert coaching, exposure to competitive football, and the opportunity to train in a professional environment, our alumni have consistently shown that FCV is a springboard for pursuing dreams in football. For more information on our football pathways, please click HERE.

Course Inclusions

Our program offers a comprehensive range of inclusions to ensure players receive the best possible football experience. Here’s an overview of what’s included:

  • In-house full-time coaching program with UEFA-qualified coaches, including specialist goalkeeping sessions
  • Exclusive accommodation with a private bathroom
  • 3 meals per day
  • Training & matchday kit
  • Competitive fixtures, friendlies with pro clubs, and showcase match opportunities
  • Filming of matches with post-match analysis
  • Specific strength and conditioning sessions
  • Fitness monitoring and testing
  • Sports injury support
  • Nutrition, health, and lifestyle advice
  • Monthly trips to live football games and various other excursions
  • Support with exit pathways and references

How to Join

  1. Contact Us: Call, email or complete our online enquiry form, providing details about yourself along with your football aspirations.
  2. Meeting: Our team will conduct a virtual meeting to learn more about you and share more details about us to ensure that FCV is the right fit for you.
  3. Application: Following on from your meeting with our admissions team, we will send you bookings forms to complete.
  4. Acceptance: Once your booking forms have been completed, your journey of football development in England will commence.
