Football Academy Trials in the UK

At FCV, we are passionate about creating opportunities for aspiring footballers to showcase their talent at the highest level. Our academy is designed to give players the exposure they need to catch the eye of professional scouts and coaches from top football clubs.

Our proven track record of helping young athletes sign for professional football clubs speaks for itself. Below are some of the various professional clubs our players have represented.

Our international approach ensures that every player, no matter where they’re from, receives the same high standard of coaching and development opportunities. We understand the challenges that come with playing in a new country, and we provide full support both on and off the field to help you settle in and focus on your football.

Football Trial Opportunities

For those players who excel and demonstrate their ability at the highest level, we are committed to taking the next step in their football journey. If you’re good enough, we will actively push you forward for trials and opportunities with professional clubs. Over the years, many of our players have gone on to trial with top teams and secure contracts with professional clubs in the UK and across Europe.

Our strong network of club connections allows us to give players a real shot at achieving their dream of playing professionally. We ensure that every standout player is given the chance to prove themselves in front of the right people, providing the exposure needed to take their career to the next level.

Football and Education to Professional Deal

A shining example of what we can offer is Milai Perott. The Bermudian goalkeeper signed a professional contract with Notts County FC after developing through FCV. He joined us in 2021, playing for our Category 1 team and impressing on both domestic and international levels. His exceptional performances earned him a call-up to Bermuda’s Under-20 squad and eventually led to a professional opportunity at Notts County. This exemplifies FCV’s commitment to nurturing talent and providing pathways for trials and signings with professional clubs.

No matter where you’re from, FCV is committed to helping you reach your full potential. Our academy is more than just a place to train—it’s a launching pad for your footballing career. If you believe you have what it takes, we are here to help you take that next crucial step towards professional football.

How To Join FCV Football Academy

Joining FCV Academy offers several tailored options:

  • Football with Education: Combine academic studies with professional football training, balancing education and sport.
  • Football Only: Focus entirely on football with intensive training, ideal for aspiring professional players.
  • Degree Courses: Pursue higher education while receiving top-level football training.
  • Summer Development Camps: Develop your skills over the summer with high-quality training and experienced coaches.

Apply Now

Our admissions team will assist you through every step of the application process to ensure you have all the information and details on what is required to join FCV International Football Academy.

  1. Contact Us: Call, email or complete our online enquiry form, providing details about yourself along with your football and academic aspirations.
  2. Interview: Our team will conduct a virtual interview to learn more about you and ensure that FCV is the right fit for your goals.
  3. Application: Following on from your meeting with our admissions team, we will send you an application form the complete.
  4. Acceptance: Once accepted, you’ll embark on an extraordinary journey that combines football excellence and academic achievement.
